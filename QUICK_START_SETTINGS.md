## Troubleshooting

### Settings Not Saving?
- They auto-save when you tap them
- No manual save needed
- Check if you see the toggle change

### Push-To-Talk Not Working?
- Make sure it's enabled (red toggle)
- Try pressing and HOLDING the button
- Not a quick tap - must be held down

### No Audio Output?
- Check Real-Time Speak is enabled (red toggle)
- Check device volume is not muted
- Check device speaker is working

### Settings Look Different?
- That's normal - premium dark theme
- Red (#FF6B6B) for enabled states
- Gray (#4A4A4A) for disabled states
- White text on black background
